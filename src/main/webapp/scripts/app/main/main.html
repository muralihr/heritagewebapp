TOWN & VILLAGE


With it's distinctive mix of Tamil tradition and French colonial legacy, urban Pondicherry is a unique Indian treasure.

But its villages are also special places with thriving cultural traditions. This map charts village heritage with the same care and attention as it devotes to living traditions within the city.
